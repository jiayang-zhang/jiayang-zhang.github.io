content_zh: |
  当我把注意力投向某个人、某个空间、某种状态时，我其实是在主动调频，让自己的频率去匹配对方的频率。注意力的方向，就是调频的方向。
  
  接收一个人的信号、感受一个空间、沉浸在某种环境里，这些行为本质上都是同一件事：把自己的频率调向对方的频率。
  
  当这件事是无意识发生的，当我没有觉察，或者内部很累调不出来，就会被外界的频率带走，非常耗。因为它不是我的。就像变成了一个接收器，别人的情绪、担忧、状态，都悄悄流进来，变成自己的负担。
  
  但有的时候，主动调频可以变成一件好事。
  
  ##### <br> **如何借助别人的频率找到自己**

  我身边有一个人，状态非常轻盈，想事情快速、简单、准确。

  我是怎么理解ta的轻的？是因为我调入了ta的频率，带着那份轻去理解，慢慢揣摩，然后发现自己内部也可以生出同样的东西。

  这个过程分四步：

  第一步，调入。把注意力真正放到对方那里，感受和思考对方的状态，接收这种频率。

  第二步，揣摩。不是分析，是让那个频率在自己身上慢慢沉淀，理解它的质地，感受它。

  第三步，浮现。内部会给出信号，找到自己也可以生出同样东西的源头。

  第四步，装上。把这份感觉装在自己身上，用的时候拿出来，不需要的时候也可以卸掉。

  借助别人的频率找到路，然后发现那条路自己也有入口。就像音叉，先让对方的震动，自己开始共鸣，慢慢调自己的某根弦。


  ##### <br> **为什么可以这样**

  我想借用一句话。

  你自己是光，想要亮的人看到你，他自会亮，不想要亮的人，不接。你最多是背景，是镜子，照他。

  所以我们也不要有负担，别人作为镜子，照亮了我们。我们也可以作为镜子，照亮别人。

  ##### <br> **留有边界的觉察，和完全被吸进去**

  这两个状态，对应了之前说的新的感觉和混沌。

  当能量充足，我自然而然地留有边界的觉察，感受到的一切更真实、更清晰、更扎根。

  当能量不足，这是一种混沌，看一切都雾蒙蒙的，如雾里看花。

  这同样适用于人、事、物。

  执着心上来，太想完成一件事，工作到了晚上，一天的能量已经耗光，但执着心压过了觉察，反而越做越乱，越容易出错。太想尽快发一些东西，这快而不稳，这时候需要回到初心和源头，重新矫正。太想要或者买某样东西，这些正是频率被拉走的信号。
  
  完全被吸进去，是一种耗，也是一种雾蒙蒙的状态，不真实。
  
  怎么跳出来？在脑海中建立一个空间，这个空间隔开我和那个人、事、物。我在一边，ta在另一边，然后告诉自己：这不是我的，这是ta的。
  
  对方有对方的课题和频率，我有我的。边界清晰，我不承担对方的，对方也不承担我的。
  
  这个抽离的过程有时候不容易，可能还会一时想回到旧的状态。但那只是一时的。抽离，回到自己，回到那个清澈、真实的状态。
  
  ##### <br> **低谷时为什么跳不出来**
  当自己不在状态，觉得疲惫、内耗、想法杂乱，自己的信号本来就弱。这时候注意力一飘出去，就很容易被别人的频道覆盖，接到他们的焦虑、情绪、节奏，然后以为那是自己的感受。
  
  乱和不稳，其实不是自己出了问题，是混频了。
  
  想调入更高、更滋养的频率，但调频本身需要能量。能量越低，越难锁定，越难维持。
  
  所以那种状态下跳不出来，不是意志力的问题，是能量的限制。
  
  出口不是"更努力地调入好的频率"，而是先补能量。等有了足够的能量，才能回到自己的频率，不被外界带着走。
  
  ##### <br> **怎么补能量**
  睡好觉。吃干净、有天然的食物。晒太阳、接触自然。身边只留好人好事。清洁、清理自己的生活状态。
  
  有些地方住起来感觉能量高，有些空间让人莫名其妙地好。这不是心理作用，这些地方携带着自己的频率。在那里，不需要努力，环境自动帮你调。
  
  睡前睡后少看手机，少刷内容，因为那些都是别人的频道，能量低的时候很容易被带跑。
  
  清洁生活状态，是保护自己的调频能力。
  
  旅行对我的意义也在这里，用更高的频率帮自己从低能量的状态里调整回来。
  
  还有独处，没有别人的频道，自己的信号才能恢复。不需要做什么，就是一个人待着。
  
  做让自己"回来"的事。每个人不一样，有人是听音乐，有人是洗澡，有人是做饭，有人是发呆。
  
  这些不是秘密，不是玄学。你其实早就知道，只是没有语言来描述它，让它落地。
  
  ##### <br> **检查自己状态**

  可以检查自己的能量状态，就问自己一句：我现在几成满？

  让自己回答，不是头脑分析。





content_en: |

  When I direct my attention toward a person or a space, I am actively tuning in — adjusting my frequency to match theirs. Wherever attention goes, tuning happens.

  Recieving the information from someone, sensing the atmosphere of a room, immersing myself in an environment — these are all the same thing at their core: shifting my frequency toward another frequency.

  When this happens unconsciously, without awareness, or when I am too tired to hold my own frequency, I get carried away by the frequencies around me. It is exhausting. Because none of it is mine. I become a receiver, and other people's emotions, worries, and states quietly flow in and become my burden. 

  But sometimes, tuning in on purpose can be a good thing.

  ##### <br> **How to find yourself through someone else's frequency**

  I have met someone whose energy is super light. The way they think is quick, simple, accurate.

  How did I come to understand that lightness? I tuned into their frequency, carried that lightness with me, and sat with it. Eventually I found that I could generate something similar from within myself. 

  The process has four steps.

  First, tune in. Put your attention genuinely on the other person. Receive their state, their frequency.

  Second, sit with it. Not analyse — just let that frequency settle in you slowly. Feel its texture. Get to know it.

  Third, let it surface. Something inside will signal back. You find the place in yourself that can generate the same thing.

  Fourth, put it on. Carry that feeling with you. Take it out when you need it, set it down when you don't.

  You use someone else's frequency to find the path, and then you discover the path has an entrance in you too. Like a tuning fork — their vibration starts, yours begins to resonate, and slowly one of your own strings comes into tune.


  ##### <br> **Why this works**
  I want to borrow something someone once said.

  You are light. Those who want to shine will see you and find their own light. Those who don't, won't receive it. At most, you are a mirror, reflecting them back to themselves.

  Others can be mirrors for us. And we can be mirrors for others.

  ##### <br> **Aware with boundaries, versus completely absorbed**
  

  These two states correspond to what I described in earlier pieces as the open feeling and the foggy state.

  When I have enough energy, I naturally stay aware while keeping a boundary. Everything feels more real, more clear, more grounded.

  When I don't have enough energy, everything turns foggy. Like trying to see through mist.

  This applies equally to people, situations, and things.

  When I am fixated on finishing something, working late into the evening with nothing left in reserve, that fixation overrides my awareness and things get messier, not cleaner. When I am rushing to put something out, moving fast without being steady, that is the signal to stop and return to the source. When I badly want something — an object, an outcome — that wanting is itself a sign my frequency has been pulled away.

  Being completely absorbed is draining. It is also not real. It is a foggy, blurred version of experience.

  So how do you step out? I create a space in my mind — one that separates me from the person, situation, or thing. I am on one side. They are on the other. And I tell myself: this is not mine. This belongs to them.
  
  They have their own lessons and their own frequency. I have mine. Clear boundary. I do not carry theirs, they do not carry mine.

  The process of stepping back is not always easy. Part of you may want to return to the old state. But that passes. Step out, return to yourself, return to that clear and real place.

  ##### <br> **Why it is hard to get out when you are in a low state**
  When I am not doing well — tired, drained, thoughts scattered — my own signal is already weak. The moment my attention drifts outward, it is easy to get swept into someone else's channel, picking up their anxiety, their mood, their pace, and mistaking it for my own.
  
  The chaos and instability is not a sign that something is wrong with me. It is crossed signals.
  
  I want to tune into something higher, something more nourishing. But tuning in takes energy. The lower my energy, the harder it is to lock on and stay there.
  
  So not being able to get out is not a willpower problem. It is an energy problem.

  The way out is not to try harder to tune into better frequencies. It is to restore energy first. Once there is enough, returning to my own frequency — and staying there without being pulled away — becomes possible again.

  ##### <br> **How to restore energy**
  Sleep well. Eat clean, natural food. Get sunlight, spend time in nature. Keep only good people and good things close. Clean up and simplify your living environment.

  Some places feel energising to be in. This is not imagination — those places carry their own frequency. Being there does the work for you. You do not have to try. You simply arrive and start receiving.

  Avoid your phone first thing in the morning and last thing at night. Limit how much content you consume. Those are other people's, and when your energy is low, you get pulled along easily.

  Keeping your environment clean is not fussiness. It is protecting your ability to tune.

  This is also what travel means to me — using a higher frequency environment to recalibrate when my own has dropped. 
  
  The same goes for solitude. Without other people, your own signal has space to return. You do not need to do anything. Just be alone for a while.

  Do whatever brings you back to yourself. It is different for everyone. Music, a shower, cooking, sitting quietly and doing nothing.

  None of this is a secret. None of it is mysterious. You already know it. You just may not have had words for it until now.
